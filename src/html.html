The story of The Regressed Genius Player’s Mythical Weapon Creation introduces Kim Si-moon, a Level 1 alchemist cursed with magic incapability. On the day Earth was on the brink of destruction, Si-moon successfully created the Philosopher’s Stone—a miraculous object that can achieve anything through equivalent exchange. “Anything is possible, you say? Then what I want right now is to strike back at the one who destroyed Earth and killed my younger sibling, and to live a life free from being powerless!” As his regret-filled life seemingly comes to an end, Si-moon unexpectedly wakes up 11 years in the past. “This time… I’ll change everything.” Determined to rewrite his tragic past as a powerless alchemist, Si-moon teams up with the Philosopher’s Stone to alter the future, fix all misfortunes, and build a life he truly deserves.
